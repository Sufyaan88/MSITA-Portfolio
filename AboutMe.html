About Me

Hi, I'm Sufyaan and I am a web developer based in Cape Town, South Africa. I'm currently a student at the College of Cape Town, studying web development. I'm aiming to develop expertise in a number of areas over the course of my life and future career. Afterall the tech industry is all about being versatile.

I am a full stack web developer and have a good understanding of the concepts of frontend development, I've also dabbled in user interface and user experience design and understand their principles fairly well. I'm also keen on doing backend development and strive to handle the entire stack as efficiently as possible.

Fueled by high levels of curiosity and boudless enthusiasm, I'm easily inspired and more than willing to follow my fascinations wherever they take me. I'm never satisfied to just come up with ideas. Instead I have an almost impulsive need to go out of my way and act on them. I prefer to keep learning, continue challenging myself, and do interesting things.

My work is something I do with alot of honesty, appetite and commitment. Through the many projects I have undertaken, I have gained valuable experiences in many fields of design and data related areas. I'm a fast learner, able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects and roles with relative ease.

My philosophy is to put the user first. Users that have a good experience are more likely to complete the actions that lead to your business goals being met. Maintain a deep understanding of foundational technology and rely on tools and frameworks where they will best serve your requirements. This leads to an efficient website and allows for good performance and user experience.
