Editing Work:
Skin Retouching


This was the first of many Photoshop tutorials that I had to work through which taught me how to edit unwanted elements out of an image and replace them with other elements to give off an appearance that the image is unchanged. For this tutorial I had to remove acne as well as other skin blemishes from a girls face by replacing those areas with good skin tones from other parts of the face.

Editing Work:
Wedding Layer Mask


During the second tutorial I learned how to use Adobe Photoshop's Layer Mask. Layer Mask's basically allow you to control a portion of an images transparency giving you the ability of making multiple images become one image without any apparent overlapping layers. For this tutorial I had to join two images of a couples wedding day photos and make them seamlessly into one.
